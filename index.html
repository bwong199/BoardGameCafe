About Box Car Cafe

Situated in the Radio Block heritage building in the heart of Victoria Park, Boxcar is one of Calgary's first board game cafes.

Created by three young board game enthusiasts with diverse backgrounds, Boxcar aims to deliver a social experience that everyone can enjoy. From friends who have never played a board game to the most veteran enthusiasts, we hope to delight you all with our large and ever growing collection of unique board games.


We are hiring for all Boxcar positions including board game experts/servers and coffee-loving baristas. If you are interested in applying, please send your cover letter and resume to info@boadgamecafe.ca. We look forward to getting you on board!
